Common Limits A few common limits are listed below:
  1. \( \lim\limits_{x\to 0} \frac{sin(x)}{x}=1 \)
  2. \( \lim\limits_{x\to 0} \frac{1-cos(x)}{x}=0 \)
  3. \( \lim\limits_{x\to 0} \frac{sin(ax)}{x}=a \)
  4. \( \lim\limits_{x\to 0} \frac{sin(ax)}{bx}=\frac{a}{b} \)
  5. \( \lim\limits_{x\to 1} \frac{ln(x)}{x-1}=1 \)
  6. \( \lim\limits_{x\to +\infty} (1+\frac{1}{x})^x=e \)
