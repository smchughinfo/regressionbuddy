Properties of Real Numbers The following properties apply for any real numbers \(a\), \(b\), and \(c\): Commutative properties of addition and multiplication \(a+b=b+a\)
\(ab=ba\) Associative properties of addition and multiplication \((a+b)+c=a+(b+c)\)
\((ab)c=a(bc)\) Distributive property \(a(b+c)=ab+bc\) Identity properties of addition and multiplication \( a+0=a \)
\( a\cdot1=a \) Inverse properties of addition and multiplication \( a-a=0 \)
\( a\cdot\frac{1}{a}=1 \)
