Cofunction Identities

The six Cofunction Identities are:

 /images/trigonometry-appendix/cofunction-definition.svg \( sin(\theta)=cos(90°\!\!-\theta) \) \( tan(\theta)=cot(90°\!\!-\theta) \) \( sec(\theta)=csc(90°\!\!-\theta) \) \( cos(\theta)=sin(90°\!\!-\theta) \) \( cot(\theta)=tan(90°\!\!-\theta) \) \( csc(\theta)=sec(90°\!\!-\theta) \) Example 1 /images/trigonometry-appendix/cofunction-definition-57-33.svg
\( sin(33°)=cos(90°\!\!-33°)=cos(57°)\approx .54 \)
Example 2 /images/trigonometry-appendix/cofunction-definition-57-33.svg
\( tan(57°)=cot(90°\!\!-57°)=cot(33°)\approx 1.54 \)
Additional Examples
  • \( sin(90°)=cos(90°\!\!-90°)=cos(0°)=1 \)
  • \( cot(89°)=tan(90°\!\!-89°)=tan(1°)\approx .02 \)
  • \( csc(15°)=sec(90°\!\!-15°)=sec(75°)\approx 3.86 \)
