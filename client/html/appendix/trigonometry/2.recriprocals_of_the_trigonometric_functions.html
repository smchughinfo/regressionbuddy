Recriprocals of the Trigonometric Functions cosecant, secant, and contangent are recriprocals of sine, cosine, and tangent, respectively. \( csc(\theta)=\frac{1}{sin(\theta)} \) \( sec(\theta)=\frac{1}{cos(\theta)} \) \( cot(\theta)=\frac{1}{cos(\theta)} \) Example /images/trigonometry-appendix/1-sqrt3-2.svg \( sin(\theta)=\frac{1}{2} \) \( cos(\theta)=\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} \) \( tan(\theta)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}} \) \( csc(\theta)=\frac{2}{1} \) \( sec(\theta)=\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} \) \( cot(\theta)=\frac{\sqrt{3}}{1} \)
