The Trigonometric Ratios The six Trigonometric Ratios are: /images/trigonometry-appendix/basic-triangle.svg \( sin(\theta)=\frac{side\ opposite\ \theta}{hypotenuse} \) \( cos(\theta)=\frac{side\ adjacent\ to\ \theta} {hypotenuse} \) \( tan(\theta)=\frac{side\ opposite\ \theta} {side\ adjacent\ to\ \theta} \) \( csc(\theta)=\frac{hypotenuse} {side\ opposite\ \theta} \) \( sec(\theta)=\frac{hypotenuse} {side\ adjacent\ to\ \theta} \) \( cot(\theta)=\frac{side\ adjacent\ to\ \theta} {side\ opposite\ \theta} \)
