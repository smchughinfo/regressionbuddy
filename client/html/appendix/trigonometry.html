The six trigonometric ratios are:

\( sin(\theta)=\frac{side\ opposite\ \theta} {hypotenuse} \)
\( cos(\theta)=\frac{side\ adjacent\ \theta} {hypotenuse} \)
\( tan(\theta)=\frac{side\ opposite\ \theta} {side\ adjacent\ \theta} \)
